About the Byteball Cashback App

Byteball is a new generation of Distributed Ledger Technologies based on a Directed Acyclic Graph (DAG).

The native cryptocurrency of the Byteball platform are Bytes. You can find more details about Byteball here.

Mass adoption of Cryptocurrencies requires wide distribution, that's why 99% of all Bytes will be distributed for free.

One of the distribution methods is the Cashback program. You can find all details here .
